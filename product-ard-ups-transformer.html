Product Description

These transformers are specifically designed for Auto Rescue Devices (ARD) used in elevators and general UPS applications. They facilitate the conversion of battery DC power to AC power during mains failure, ensuring safe operation of critical systems.

Key Features

  • Optimized for battery operation
  • High efficiency at partial loads
  • Compact design for panel mounting
  • Thermal protection included

Applications

Elevator Auto Rescue Devices, Online/Offline UPS, Emergency Lighting Systems, Security Systems.
