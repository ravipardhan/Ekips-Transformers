Product Description

As a leading buck boost transformer supplier, we provide small, single-phase transformers designed to reduce (buck) or raise (boost) line voltage from 5-20%. They are the most efficient way to correct off-standard voltages for equipment like air conditioners, heaters, and motors.

Key Features

  • Cost-effective voltage correction
  • Compact size

  • Easy installation

HVAC systems, Motor applications, Tanning beds, Low voltage lighting, UPS systems.
