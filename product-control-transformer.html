Product Description

Control Transformers are designed to provide a high degree of secondary voltage stability (regulation) during a brief period of overload condition (also referred to as inrush current). They are typically used to power contactors, solenoids, relays, and timers in industrial panels.

Key Features

  • Excellent voltage regulation
  • High inrush current capability
  • Compact and safe design
  • Long service life

Applications

Motor Control Centers (MCC), Control Panels, Machine Tool Control, Automation Systems.
